About Us

Global India Insurance Broker’s Pvt. Ltd. presents MyPolicyNow.com, one of the fastest-growing Insurance Broking initiatives in India. This is our online chapter, a digital venture into the world of insurance. We provide complete peace of mind when it comes to the purchase and sales of insurance policies. Our infrastructure has been seamlessly integrated with peerless technology, making the transaction process easy and convenient

We specialize in motor, health and life insurances. Being associated with the top insurers in the Indian market, we are in a position to offer smart, insightful and effective information that will help you compare policies, choose from a range of options, buy the ones that cater to your needs or sell motor, health & life insurance schemes to prospective customers.
